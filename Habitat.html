Where on earth is the animal found?
Ligers exist only in captivity because the habitats of the parental species do not overlap in the wild. Historically, when the Asiatic Lion was prolific, the territories of lions and tigers did overlap and there are legends of ligers existing in the wild.

What natural surroundings does the animal need to thrive? It is estimated that a liger requires at least 30 pounds of raw meat in one day
